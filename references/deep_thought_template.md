# Analysis Workbench

## Purpose

This is NOT a mandatory form to fill out. It's a **thinking workspace** — a place to draft your thesis, test it, revise it, and track how your thinking evolves through the analysis loop.

Use whatever sections are relevant. Skip whatever isn't. Add whatever helps you think better. The goal is better decisions, not completed forms.

---

## Draft Thesis

> "I believe [ASSET] will [DIRECTION] because [CAUSAL MECHANISM]. The market is pricing in [CONSENSUS], but it's missing [KEY VARIABLE]. The catalyst is [WHAT CHANGES THE EQUILIBRIUM]. I would be wrong if [DISCONFIRMING EVENT]."

If you can't fill this out, you don't have a thesis yet. That's OK — go research, then come back.
