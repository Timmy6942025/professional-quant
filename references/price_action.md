# Price Action & Market Microstructure

## Candlestick Psychology

### Bullish Patterns
- **Hammer**: Long lower wick, small body at top → Buying pressure rejected lower prices
- **Bullish Engulfing**: Large green candle engulfs prior red → Momentum shift
- **Morning Star**: Three-candle reversal pattern → Bottom confirmation
- **Three White Soldiers**: Three consecutive strong green candles → Strong uptrend

### Bearish Patterns
- **Shooting Star**: Long upper wick, small body at bottom → Selling pressure at highs
- **Bearish Engulfing**: Large red candle engulfs prior green → Momentum shift down
- **Evening Star**: Three-candle top pattern → Top confirmation
- **Three Black Crows**: Three consecutive strong red candles → Strong downtrend

## Support & Resistance

### Identifying Key Levels
- **Horizontal S/R**: Price reversed multiple times at same level
- **Trendlines**: Connect 2+ highs (resistance) or lows (support)
- **Moving Averages**: 50 MA, 200 MA act as dynamic S/R
- **VWAP**: Volume Weighted Average Price (institutional fair value)

### S/R Strength
- More touches = stronger level
- Longer time spent at level = stronger
- Volume spike at level = institutional interest
- Breakout with volume = likely genuine

## Volume Analysis (Critical!)

### Volume Precedes Price
- **Volume spike + price breakout** = Genuine move
- **Volume divergence** (price up, volume down) = Weak move, likely to fail
- **Climax volume** (highest in months) = Exhaustion, reversal coming

### Volume Patterns
- **Accumulation**: Rising volume on up days, low volume on down days → Smart money buying
- **Distribution**: Rising volume on down days, low volume on up days → Smart money selling
- **Churn**: High volume, little price progress → Indecision, potential reversal

## Market Structure

### Trend Identification
- **Uptrend**: Higher highs + higher lows
- **Downtrend**: Lower highs + lower lows
- **Range**: Equal highs + equal lows

### Break of Structure (BOS)
- When uptrend makes lower low → Trend change
- When downtrend makes higher high → Trend change
- BOS with volume = High probability setup

## Order Flow (If Data Available)
- **Bid-Ask Spread**: Tight spread = Liquid, wide = Illiquid
- **Level 2 / Depth**: See pending orders, identify support/resistance
- **Time & Sales**: See actual execution, detect aggression

## Multiple Timeframe Analysis

### Top-Down Approach
1. **Monthly/Weekly**: Identify long-term trend
2. **Daily**: Find entry/exit zones
3. **Hourly/15min**: Fine-tune entry timing

### Confluence
- Entry signal on 3 timeframes = High probability
- Signal on 1 timeframe only = Low probability

## The "Insane" Price Action Edge

What separates good from great:

1. **Read the auction**: Markets are auctions. Is price finding fair value or seeking new levels?
2. **Volume tells truth**: Price can lie (manipulation), volume rarely does
3. **Watch the wicks**: Rejections at key levels are more important than closes
4. **Context matters**: Bullish pattern in bear market = trap. Bearish pattern in bull market = opportunity
5. **Institutional footprints**: Large volume at round numbers = Institutions defending/attacking

## Reading the Tape (Free yfinance Data)

With just OHLCV data, you can still detect:
- **Wide range bars** = Strong momentum day
- **Narrow range bars** = Compression before expansion
- **Closes near highs** = Buying pressure
- **Closes near lows** = Selling pressure
- **Doji (open≈close)** = Indecision, potential reversal

## Actionable Price Levels

Always provide to user:
- **Strong Buy Zone**: Key support + oversold RSI
- **Entry Trigger**: Specific candle pattern or breakout level
- **Stop Loss**: Below key support or ATR-based
- **Take Profit 1**: First resistance (risk:reward 1:2)
- **Take Profit 2**: Extended target (risk:reward 1:4)
